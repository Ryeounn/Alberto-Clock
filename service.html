COMMON MISTAKES IN A GENUINE WATCH

Before learning about watch repair services in Hai Trieu, let's take a look at some common errors when using watches:

Running wrong time, fast, slow: The cause comes from the clock is about to run out of battery, magnetized. If it is a mechanical watch, it may be due to not charging enough energy every day.

Stopping: This is a common error on both Quartz and Automatic watches. Most of the reason comes from the watch running out of Battery / Energy stored or the movement is dry.

Watches get into water: When used beyond water resistance, or in environments with high humidity, not turning the knob carefully, etc., it can easily lead to steaming or entering water.

Broken, cracked glass: This is a common situation when the watch face is dropped. In this case, you need to replace the glass immediately.

Scratches on the glass: Caused by improper use of the watch causing scratches. Focused mainly on hard glass and plastic glass watches.

Broken strap: Especially the leather watch strap, when used for a long time, it will lead to aging. The watch repair for this case is to replace the strap (1 - 3 years / 1 time).
